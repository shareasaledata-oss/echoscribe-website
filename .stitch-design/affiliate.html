How Links Work

When you click a link on Echos Scribes that leads to a retailer (like Amazon, Anthropologie, or specialized electronics boutiques), a small cookie is placed in your browser. This cookie simply tells the retailer that you came from our site.

If you make a purchase within a certain window of time, the retailer provides us with a small percentage of the sale. This does not change the price you pay; in fact, we often negotiate exclusive discount codes for our readers that you wouldn't find elsewhere.

Editorial Independence

Our editorial team operates with complete autonomy. We do not accept payment in exchange for positive reviews, nor do we let affiliate potential dictate which products we choose to feature.

Our Promise

We only recommend products that align with the Echos Scribes aesthetic of "Minimalist-Organic" living—items that are as functional as they are beautiful. If we wouldn't put it in our own homes or gardens, we won't suggest it for yours.

Your trust is our most valuable asset. If you ever have questions about our partnerships or how we review products, we invite you to reach out to our editorial board directly via our contact page.
