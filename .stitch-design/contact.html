Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you might have.

How long does shipping take? expand_more
Our standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days for domestic orders and 7-14 business days for international destinations. Every package is carefully wrapped in our signature sustainable packaging.
Do you offer international returns? expand_more
Yes, we accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Please note that for international returns, shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item arrived damaged.
Can I change my subscription plan? expand_more
Absolutely. You can manage your subscription directly through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
