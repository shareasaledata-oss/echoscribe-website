# What Is DIY Fashion and Upcycling?

DIY fashion stands for “Do It Yourself” fashion. It involves creating, modifying, or decorating clothing and accessories without relying on professional designers or manufacturers.

Upcycling is the process of reusing old clothing or materials to create something new and more valuable.

Unlike recycling, which often breaks materials down into raw components, upcycling preserves the original fabric while improving or redesigning it.

Examples include:

-   Turning old jeans into shorts
-   Adding embroidery to jackets
-   Converting oversized shirts into crop tops
-   Creating tote bags from old fabric
-   Decorating sneakers with paint or patches

DIY fashion encourages creativity and individuality.
