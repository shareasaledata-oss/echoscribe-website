# What Are Home Spa and Self-Care Routines?

A home spa routine involves recreating the relaxing and therapeutic experience of a professional spa within the comfort of your own home.

Self-care routines are intentional activities that improve physical, emotional, and mental well-being.

These routines may include:

-   Skincare treatments
-   Aromatherapy
-   Meditation
-   Relaxation baths
-   Hair care
-   Massage therapy
-   Mindfulness exercises

The goal is to create moments of calmness, recovery, and personal wellness.
