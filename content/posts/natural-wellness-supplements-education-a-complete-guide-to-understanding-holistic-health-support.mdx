# What Are Natural Wellness Supplements?

Natural wellness supplements are products designed to support overall health and well-being using naturally sourced ingredients.

They may include:

-   Vitamins
-   Minerals
-   Herbal extracts
-   Plant compounds
-   Probiotics
-   Amino acids
-   Omega fatty acids

Supplements are intended to complement a healthy lifestyle rather than replace nutritious food or medical care.

Natural supplements are often used to support:

-   Energy levels
-   Immune health
-   Sleep quality
-   Digestion
-   Stress management
-   General wellness

# The Difference Between Supplements and Medicine

Supplements and medications are not the same.

## Supplements

![](/images/blog/pexels-n-voitkevich-7615571-1024x683.jpg)

Supplements are designed to support normal body functions and wellness.

They are generally used as part of preventive or supportive health practices.

## Medications

Medications are intended to diagnose, treat, or manage medical conditions.

They are usually prescribed or recommended for specific health concerns.

Supplements should not replace professional medical treatment when needed.
